Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is made with eggs, cheese, bacon, and black pepper. It's creamy, rich, and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Bacon or pancetta
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  3. Cook the bacon or pancetta until crispy, then drain on paper towels.
  4. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it and add it to the bowl with the egg mixture.
  5. Toss everything together until the spaghetti is coated in the creamy sauce.
  6. Crumble the cooked bacon or pancetta on top and serve immediately.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup (65g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 cup (240ml) whole milk, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) boiling water

For the frosting:

  • 1 cup (230g) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 3 and 1/2 cups (420g) powdered sugar, sifted
  • 1/2 cup (45g) unsweetened cocoa powder, sifted
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) heavy cream or milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans, or line them with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Beat on medium speed with a hand mixer or in a stand mixer until well combined, about 2 minutes.
  4. Reduce the mixer speed to low and carefully add the boiling water to the cake batter. Beat on low speed until just combined. The batter will be thin, but that's okay.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.
  7. Remove the cakes from the oven and allow them to cool in the pans for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. While the cakes are cooling, prepare the frosting. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter on medium speed until creamy, about 2 minutes.
  9. Gradually add the powdered sugar, cocoa powder, and salt to the butter, mixing on low speed until combined. Add the vanilla extract and heavy cream, then increase the mixer speed to medium-high and beat until the frosting is light and fluffy, about 3-4 minutes.
  10. Once the cakes are completely cooled, frost the top of one cake layer with a generous amount of frosting. Place the second cake layer on top and frost the top and sides of the cake with the remaining frosting.
  11. Slice, serve, and enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate cake!

Biryani

Ingredients:

For Marination:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

For Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • Water as needed
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2-3 green cardamom
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste

For Biryani:

  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron strands (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons warm milk (optional)

Directions:

Marinate the Chicken:

  • In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt.
  • Add chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour, preferably longer in the refrigerator.

    • Prepare the Rice:

  • In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add soaked rice, bay leaf, cloves, green cardamom, cinnamon stick, and salt.
  • Cook until the rice is 70-80% done. Drain the water and set the rice aside.

    • Prepare Biryani:

  • Heat ghee or oil in a large skillet or pot. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions and set aside for garnish.
  • To the remaining onions in the pot, add marinated chicken pieces. Cook until the chicken is partially cooked and the yogurt mixture thickens.
  • Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken evenly. Sprinkle chopped tomatoes, green chilies, coriander leaves, and mint leaves over the rice.
  • Drizzle saffron-infused warm milk (optional) over the rice for added flavor and color.
  • Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked and the rice is done.

    • Serve:

  • Once done, gently mix the layers of chicken and rice together.
  • Garnish with the reserved fried onions
  • Serve hot with raita (yogurt-based dip) or salan (spicy gravy) on the side.

    chicken rice

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups of jasmine rice
    • 4 cups of chicken broth
    • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs or breasts
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 1-inch piece of ginger, sliced
    • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
    • Soy sauce, for serving
    • Sliced cucumbers and tomatoes, for garnish (optional)
    • Chili sauce or chili oil, for serving (optional)

    Instructions:

    1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain well.
    2. In a large pot or rice cooker, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and ginger slices, and sauté until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
    3. Add the rice to the pot and stir to coat with the garlic and ginger. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    4. Pour in the chicken broth and season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
    5. While the rice is cooking, season the chicken thighs or breasts with salt and pepper.
    6. In a separate pan, heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces skin-side down and cook until golden brown, about 5-6 minutes. Flip the chicken and continue cooking until cooked through, about another 5-6 minutes. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
    7. Once the rice is done, fluff it with a fork and serve alongside the sliced chicken. Garnish with sliced cucumbers and tomatoes if desired, and serve with soy sauce and chili sauce or chili oil on the side.

    burger

    Ingredients:

    • 1 pound (450g) ground beef (preferably 80/20 lean to fat ratio)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 4 hamburger buns
    • Your choice of toppings: lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, etc.

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
    2. Divide the ground beef into four equal portions and gently shape each portion into a patty about 1/2 to 3/4 inch thick. Press a slight indentation into the center of each patty with your thumb to prevent it from bulging while cooking. Season both sides of the patties generously with salt and pepper.
    3. Place the patties on the grill or grill pan and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or until desired level of doneness is reached. Avoid pressing down on the patties with a spatula while they cook, as this can cause the juices to escape and result in a less juicy burger.
    4. Place the patties on the grill or grill pan and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or until desired level of doneness is reached. Avoid pressing down on the patties with a spatula while they cook, as this can cause the juices to escape and result in a less juicy burger.
    5. Once the burgers are cooked to your liking, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
    6. Assemble your burgers by placing the cooked patties on the bottom halves of the hamburger buns. Add your desired toppings, such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise
    7. Top with the remaining halves of the hamburger buns and serve immediately.

    ice cream

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups heavy cream
    • 1 cup whole milk
    • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
    • Pinch of salt
    • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 4 ounces (about 120g) bittersweet or semisweet chocolate, chopped
    • 4 large egg yolks
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    Instructions:

    1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, salt, and cocoa powder. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it reaches a simmer. Remove from heat.
    2. Add the chopped chocolate to the hot cream mixture and stir until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture is smooth.
    3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks until smooth. Slowly pour about 1/2 cup of the hot chocolate mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly to temper the eggs and prevent them from curdling.
    4. Gradually whisk the egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining chocolate mixture.
    5. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon, about 5-7 minutes. Do not let it boil.
    6. Remove the saucepan from the heat and strain the custard through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl to remove any cooked egg bits.
    7. Stir in the vanilla extract.
    8. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, pressing it directly onto the surface of the custard to prevent a skin from forming. Chill the custard in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight until completely cold.
    9. Once the custard is chilled, churn it in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturer's instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency, about 20-25 minutes.
    10. Transfer the churned ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
    11. Serve your homemade chocolate ice cream scooped into bowls or cones, or use it to make ice cream sandwiches, floats, or sundaes.
