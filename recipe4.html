chicken rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of jasmine rice
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs or breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Soy sauce, for serving
  • Sliced cucumbers and tomatoes, for garnish (optional)
  • Chili sauce or chili oil, for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain well.
  2. In a large pot or rice cooker, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and ginger slices, and saut√© until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the rice to the pot and stir to coat with the garlic and ginger. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  5. While the rice is cooking, season the chicken thighs or breasts with salt and pepper.
  6. In a separate pan, heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces skin-side down and cook until golden brown, about 5-6 minutes. Flip the chicken and continue cooking until cooked through, about another 5-6 minutes. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  7. Once the rice is done, fluff it with a fork and serve alongside the sliced chicken. Garnish with sliced cucumbers and tomatoes if desired, and serve with soy sauce and chili sauce or chili oil on the side.
